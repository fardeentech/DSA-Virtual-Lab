Binary search tree

Estimated Time

1 hour

Learning Objectives of the Experiment

  • Understand the basic concepts of tree data structures, including nodes, edges, and levels.
  • Implement a binary tree and perform fundamental operations such as insertion, deletion, and traversal (in-order, pre-order, and post-order).
  • Analyze the time complexity of various tree operations and understand their impact on performance.
  • Compare binary trees with other tree structures, such as binary search trees (BSTs) and AVL trees, and understand their applications.
  • Explore tree-based algorithms and their applications in solving problems like searching, sorting, and expression evaluation.
