Prerequisites of the Experiment

This experiment requires you to have basic knowledge about:

  • The Concept of Data Structures
  • Understanding of LIFO (Last In, First Out) Principle
  • Notion of Time and Space Complexity
  • A passion for problem-solving and learning!

Overview of the Experiment

The aim of this experiment is to understand the Stack data structure, its operations, time and space complexity, and how it is used in various applications.

The experiment consists of several modules including video tutorials, interactive simulations, hands-on coding exercises, and quizzes to help you evaluate your understanding.
