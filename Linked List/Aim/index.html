Insertion Sort

Learning Objectives of the Experiment

In this experiment, we will be able to do the following:

  • Understand the fundamental concepts of linked lists and their structure compared to arrays.
  • Implement basic operations such as insertion, deletion, and traversal on a linked list.
  • Analyze the time complexity of various operations in a linked list.
  • Recognize the advantages and disadvantages of using linked lists over arrays.
  • Explore the different types of linked lists, including singly linked lists, doubly linked lists, and circular linked lists.
